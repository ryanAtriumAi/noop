# NOOP v8.2.2

A reliability and polish release. Steadier Bluetooth, a stack of fixes from your reports, more of the app translated, and a nicer sleep view. Update from the Releases page, AltStore, or `brew upgrade`.

## Steadier connections

- **A dropped strap reconnects without dropping a live one.** A stale reconnect timer could fire after your strap had already come back and tear the fresh connection down. It can't anymore. (ryanbr, #1030)
- **The heart-rate re-broadcast survives a Bluetooth toggle.** If you use NOOP to rebroadcast HR to gym kit or Zwift, turning Bluetooth off and on used to kill it until you re-toggled the setting. It now restarts itself. (ryanbr, #1029)
- **Sharper HRV.** R-R intervals are rounded to match the other paths, HRV windows need a few clean beats before they count, and readiness scoring got a little faster. (ryanbr, #1033, #1034, #1035, #1036)

## Fixes

- **Your WHOOP shows up in the Devices list on a fresh install.** It was pairing and streaming fine but never appeared in the list on a brand-new install. Fixed. (#1037)
- **A corrupt local database no longer crash-loops the app.** If your database ever got corrupted, the app could crash on every launch until a reinstall. It now sets the bad file aside, rebuilds a clean one, and opens, keeping the corrupt copy for recovery. (#1014)
- **Readable score-card labels in light mode.** The Charge / Effort / Rest labels and the WHOOP pills followed the system theme and went near-invisible on the dark card in light mode. Pinned readable in both themes, across Today and Trends, and the Today date header now shows in your language. (#1013)
- **Live HR screen no longer clips when you scroll.** (#1027)
- **macOS chart polish:** the resting-HR axis labels and the calendar year-heatmap squares no longer crop. (#1019, #1021)

## More of NOOP in your language

- **Effort, Charge, BPM and a few other core labels now translate.** They were hardcoded English and skipped translation everywhere. Fixed, so the existing German (and other) translations finally apply. (#1020)
- **Portuguese (Portugal) added.** Full pt-PT across the app. Obrigado, tigercraft4. (#1018)

## A better sleep view

- **Sleep detail, redesigned.** A per-stage timeline, a sleeping heart-rate chart, and a 30-day "typical" comparison so you can see how a night stacks up. Groundwork by ryanAtriumAi. (#988)

## Stronger first-run terms

The first-launch agreement now asks you to confirm a few specific points individually rather than one blanket tick, and records when you accepted. Nothing about how the app works changes.

## Notes

Two of the above (the Bluetooth reconnect and broadcast changes) are behind-the-scenes connection handling. If anything with reconnection behaves oddly, a strap log helps us a lot.
